Our Departments

Located in the serene locality of Phutung Kathmandu, our school offers modern facilities, including well-equipped labs, music rooms, and spacious playgrounds, housed in earthquake-resistant buildings. We prioritize student success through personalized support services and a vibrant community that fosters collaboration, innovation, and social responsibility.

Library

Our library offers a diverse collection of books, journals, and resources, catering to the academic and intellectual pursuits of our students.

Whether you're delving into the pages of literary classics, exploring the latest research in science and technology, or immersing yourself in the world of fiction, our library provides a nurturing environment for learning and discovery. With comfortable seating, modern amenities, and knowledgeable staff on hand to assist you, the library is not just a place to study, but a sanctuary for exploration and growth. Come, unlock the doors to endless possibilities and let your imagination soar within the walls of our library.

Computer Lab

Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and software, our lab serves as a dynamic space for exploration, creativity, and learning.

Whether you're diving into coding and programming, delving into graphic design, or conducting research online, our computer lab provides the tools and resources you need to bring your ideas to life. With high-speed internet access, modern workstations, and expert guidance from our dedicated staff, the computer lab is more than just a room filled with machines—it's a gateway to limitless possibilities and future success in the digital age. Join us and embark on a journey of discovery and innovation in our computer lab.

Fields for Sports

Our meticulously maintained fields provide the perfect backdrop for students to engage in a variety of sports and physical activities, fostering a healthy and active lifestyle.

Whether it's soccer, basketball, volleyball, or track and field, our expansive outdoor spaces cater to a diverse range of interests and skill levels. With dedicated coaches and modern facilities, including equipment and training resources, students have the opportunity to develop their athletic abilities, build camaraderie, and strive for excellence on and off the field. Join us in embracing the joy of sportsmanship and competition as we celebrate the power of teamwork and dedication in our fields of play.

Extracurricular Activities

Designed to complement academic learning, our ECA offerings provide students with opportunities to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lifelong friendships.

From arts and culture clubs to debate teams, robotics competitions, and community service projects, our diverse range of activities caters to every interest and personality. With dedicated mentors and modern facilities, students have the support and resources they need to excel in their chosen pursuits and make a meaningful impact in their communities. Join us in embracing the spirit of creativity, collaboration, and personal growth through our vibrant ECA program.

Science Lab

Equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and resources, our lab provides students with a hands-on learning experience that sparks curiosity and ignites a passion for scientific exploration.

From conducting experiments to analyzing data and drawing conclusions, our lab offers students the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios. With guidance from experienced instructors and safety protocols in place, students can delve into the realms of biology, chemistry, physics, and beyond, fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a deeper understanding of the natural world.
